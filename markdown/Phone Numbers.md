title: Phone Numbers

## Flags

> Flags: `(?i-Ums)`

> * -U: Greedy
> * -m: Do not treat target as one line
> * -s: Dot does not match newline
> * i: Case insensitive

## Regular Expression Search

```ruby
/(?i-Ums)^(?:(?:\+?1\s*(?:[.-]\s*)?)?(?:\(\s*([2-9]1[02-9]|[2-9][02-8]1|[2-9][02-8][02-9])\s*\)|([2-9]1[02-9]|[2-9][02-8]1|[2-9][02-8][02-9]))\s*(?:[.-]\s*)?)?([2-9]1[02-9]|[2-9][02-9]1|[2-9][02-9]{2})\s*(?:[.-]\s*)?([0-9]{4})(?:\s*(?:#|x\.?|ext\.?|extension)\s*(\d+))?$/
```
